Brief Bio

I am a senior student majoring in Data Science and Big Data Technology at Northeast Forestry University. I served as a research assistant in the humanoid robot laboratory, and studied and worked under the guidance of Professor Chen Yu for two and a half years. At the same time, I served as a research assistant in the "Heterogeneous Network" (uncertain name) laboratory, and worked under the guidance of Professor Wang Mingyang for one and a half years. I have participated in many practical projects and research during my four years of study and work. My main research interests include machine learning, data mining, big data analysis, and robot motion control. I led my team to win more than ten national awards in multiple robot competitions in 2021 and 2022. At the same time, I also focus on software development and have rich experience in front-end and back-end development. I have won the Best Web Award twice in international competitions. And I am familiar with the Hadoop ecosystem and have rich experience in deploying, optimizing, operating and maintaining Hadoop clusters, data processing, and machine learning.
